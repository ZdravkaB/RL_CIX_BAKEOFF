The Tradition

Most Tuesdays, our team trades spreadsheets for spatulas at 50 New Bond Street, London. The Global CIX Bake Off is a celebration of patience, generosity and a little friendly competition — a moment to slow down, savour something handmade, and applaud the maker.

Whether you are a seasoned pâtissier or a first-time baker, there is a place for you at the table. Register, bake, and let your colleagues crown the week's favourite.

House Rules

The rules of
the table

A few simple principles keep the Global CIX Bake Off gracious, safe and fair for everyone who takes part.

01

Most Tuesdays

The Global CIX Bake Off takes place most Tuesdays throughout the season, held after the CIX meeting when there is one, or at another convenient time when there isn't.

02

Bake anything you like, to enjoy

Participants may bake any type of cake, sweet or savoury, the choice is entirely yours. Employees must participate at least once during the season to be allowed to enjoy the desserts.

03

Allergens, always

Bring a complete ingredients and allergen list on the day of the event, for everyone's safety. Please clearly flag common allergens such as gluten, nuts, peanuts, dairy, eggs, soy, and shellfish.
